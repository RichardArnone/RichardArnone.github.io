Graphic Design

I believe that before your first sketch, you need to understand who your client is and how you can help them achieve their goal. Understanding the needs of your client is the first step towards a successful design.

I have acquired extensive knowledge and practical skills with various Adobe Creative Suite programs; namely, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. With these tools I am able to take concepts and make them a reality.

Front End Development

It is always exciting to see your designs come to life, especially when it allows for user interaction. With front end development, you are providing the first impression of what is to be expected. You set the mood for the entirety of the experience.

Coding various websites, landing pages and email communications, has allowed me to hone my skills in HTML5, CSS3, Java Script, jQuery and various content management systems.
