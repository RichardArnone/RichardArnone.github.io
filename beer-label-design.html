Beer Label Design

Ganaraska Brewing Company

I was connected with Ganaraska Brewing Company through a mutual contact who informed me they were in need of a refresh. The client wanted to utilize a comic book/cartoon style for their label artwork. I began conceptualizing how their current labels could take on a new identity while opening the door for future labels to follow suit. The client was thrilled with the turn out of the labels, prompting an on-going, working relationship that remains to this day. Below you can explore the labels themselves as well as some applications of the illustrated artwork.
