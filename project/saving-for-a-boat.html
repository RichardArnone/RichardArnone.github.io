Saving for a Boat

ALL WORK

The Grimsby Auxiliary Marine Rescue Unit is an Ontario not-for-profit organization dedicated to saving lives and protecting property on the water; so when they needed to be rescued themselves, there was no question. I designed a bi-fold broschure for them to use in hopes of receiving funds for a new rescue vessel.
