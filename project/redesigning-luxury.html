Brookhouse Gate is a planned luxury condominum in Newcastle, Ontario. Trouble selling off the last of their suites put me in the position to refresh their online presence in an effort to attract potential buyers looking for a new home. Steering away from the previous fixed width style, I designed a full width theme for the site to help modernize its appearance on desktop and mobile devices.
